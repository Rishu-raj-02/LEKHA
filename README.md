## 🚀 What is Lekha?

**Lekha** is a mobile-first SaaS solution designed specifically for Indian shopkeepers (Kirana stores, local boutiques, and small service providers). It transforms the traditional "Bahi Khatta" (physical ledger) into a powerful, digital business companion. 

Lekha was built to bridge the gap between complex enterprise software and manual, error-prone paper bookkeeping.

---

## ❌ The Problem

Many small businesses in India still rely on manual ledgers, which leads to:
* **Manual Udhar Errors**: Forgetting to record a credit entry means lost money.
* **Lack of Tracking**: Difficult to see who owes what at a glance.
* **Limited Insights**: No easy way to know daily profits or top-selling items.
* **Complex Tools**: existing ERP/POS systems are too complicated for small mobile users.

---

## ✅ The Solution

Lekha provides a streamlined, bilingual (Hindi & English), and mobile-responsive interface that simplifies daily operations:
* ⚡ **Instant Billing**: Create and share professional digital bills in seconds.
* 💰 **Udhar Tracking**: A dedicated ledger to track pending and paid credits.
* 📲 **WhatsApp Integration**: Send one-click bill summaries and reminders to customers.
* 📊 **Smart Insights**: Real-time sales trends and business performance metrics.

## ✨ Features

### 🧾 Smart Billing
* Add items from inventory or enter them manually.
* Instant total calculations.
* Generate professional digital invoices.

### 👥 Customer Management
* Maintain a digital directory of your customers.
* View purchase history and credit balance for each individual.

### 💰 Udhar Ledger
* Clear visibility into **Pending** (Red) vs. **Paid** (Green) amounts.
* Quick "Mark as Paid" functionality to keep books up to date.

### 📦 Item Inventory
* Manage your products, categories, and pricing.
* Track which items are your best sellers.

### 📊 Business Insights
* **Today's Summary**: Instantly see your earnings and pending udhar for the day.
* **7-Day Trend**: Visualize your growth with daily sales charts.
* **Ranking**: Identify your top 3 customers and most sold products.

### 📲 One-Click WhatsApp
* Share bills directly to customer phones via WhatsApp.
* Automated professional message templates in both English and Hindi.

## 📊 Highlight: Smart Analytics

Lekha doesn't just record data; it helps you grow. The **Insights** tab provides:
* **Sales Trends**: Identify which days of the week are most profitable.
* **Credit Analysis**: See your total pending collections vs. successfully received payments.
* **Smart Alerts**: Receive tips if your pending udhar is reaching critical levels.

---

## 🔐 Security & Privacy

Privacy is our priority. Lekha ensures:
* **Data Isolation**: Your data is yours alone. UID-based rules prevent cross-user access.
* **Secure Auth**: Only authenticated Google accounts can access shop data.
* **Cloud Backup**: Real-time Firestore synchronization ensures you never lose data, even if you change phones.

---

## 📱 Mobile-First Experience

Lekha is designed to work like a native app on your phone:
* **Glassmorphism UI**: Modern, translucent navigation for a premium feel.
* **Fast & Lightweight**: Lazy-loaded components for instant tab switching.
* **Offline Ready**: Local data caching ensures the app opens instantly on slow connections.
